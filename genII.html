Pokemon Generation II New Features

Time of Day

In Pokemon Generation II, the time of day is now a factor in the game. Certain events, such as the evolution of certain Pokemon, will only occur at certain times of the day.

  • Pokemon that only evolve during the day: Espeon, Umbreon
  • Pokemon that only evolve during the night: Crobat
  • Day and night exclusive Pokemon: Hoothoot, Noctowl, Murkrow, Misdreavus, Wobbuffet, Girafarig, Teddiursa, and Sneasel

New Regions

In Pokemon Generation II, two new regions are introduced: Johto and Kanto. The player starts in Johto and can later travel to Kanto to explore new areas and battle gym leaders from the previous game.

  • New Johto region: Azalea Town, Goldenrod City, Ecruteak City, Olivine City, Cianwood City, Mahogany Town, Blackthorn City
  • Returning Kanto region: Pewter City, Cerulean City, Vermilion City, Lavender Town, Fuchsia City, Saffron City, Celadon City, and Viridian City
  • Access to previously inaccessible areas, such as the Safari Zone and Mt. Silver

Breeding

In Pokemon Generation II, Pokemon breeding is introduced, allowing players to breed their Pokemon to produce offspring with new moves and abilities.

  • Players can breed Pokemon at the daycare center in Johto
  • Produced offspring can inherit moves and abilities from their parents, including moves not normally learned through leveling up or TM/HM
  • Gender plays a role in breeding, with some Pokemon being genderless or only able to breed with certain genders

Shiny Pokemon

In Pokemon Generation II, shiny Pokemon are introduced, which are rare variations of normal Pokemon with different color schemes.

  • Shiny Pokemon have a 1 in 8,192 chance of appearing in the wild
  • Shiny Pokemon have a different color scheme than their normal counterparts
